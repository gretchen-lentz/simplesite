A Few Words About Me

I am a senior business marketing student at the University of Northern Colorado. Outside of school I am involved in UNC Club Figure Skating and my sorority, Delta Zeta.

Volunteer and Community Giveback Interests

Two of my core values are genorosity and citizenship. I enjoy volunteerig in a multitude of ways and giving back to my communities. Some of my favorite ways to do this is through animal shelters, mountain area clean-up programs, and nonprofit youth sports organizations.

Figure Skating

I have been a competitive figure skater for over ten years. Since beggining this journey I have become a USFSA Gold Medalist in Moves in the Field, and earned the title of 2018 Southwestern Regional Intermediate Excel Ladies Champion. I now spend most of my time in skating working on my USFSA Ice Dane tests aned coaching group classes on the side. In the future I hope to complete my PSA certifications which will allow me to coach and choregraph for private clients.

More About Me

I am originally from Vail Colorado where I grew up on a ranch with my mom,dad, younger brother and a multitude of furry friends. Being from the mountains has instilled a huge love for all things outdoors. When I am not skating or working on school you can find me camping, hiking, and wakeboarding in the summer, and skiing and skinning in the winter.
